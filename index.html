01 — About me

The story so far

K

I started my journey writing lines of C++ that barely compiled — and somehow ended up obsessed with why buttons feel satisfying to click. I'm Kiruba, a Computer Science student specialising in UI/UX Design, and I live in the overlap between logic and aesthetics.

I believe great design is invisible — it guides, delights, and solves problems without announcing itself. Every pixel I push is a tiny decision rooted in empathy for the user and respect for clean code. When I'm not prototyping in Figma, I'm probably reverse-engineering an app I love, or sketching ideas on anything that holds ink.

2022 — The spark
Started with a dream of becoming a developer. Took my first steps into coding and exploring technology.
2023 — The beginning
Began my CSE journey. Learned programming fundamentals and built my first projects.
2024 — The shift
Discovered UI/UX design. Realised design is about solving problems with creativity.
2025 — Growth
Sharpening my skills. Working on real projects and improving both design and development.
2026 — Now
Building meaningful products at the intersection of UX research and frontend development.
